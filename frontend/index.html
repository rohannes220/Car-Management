Welcome to Car Manager

The purpose of this project is to create a car inventory and sales management system for a dealership. The platform allows dealership staff to manage vehicle inventory, update vehicle information, and track completed sales in an organized system.

System Features

  • Add new vehicles to the dealership inventory with details such as make, model, year, price, mileage, and color.
  • View all cars currently stored in the inventory database.
  • Update vehicle information such as pricing, mileage, or availability when changes occur.
  • Remove vehicles from the system once they are sold or no longer part of the dealership inventory.
  • Track completed car sales and maintain records of dealership transactions.
  • Monitor the availability status of each vehicle (available or sold).
  • Maintain an organized digital inventory system for dealership management.
